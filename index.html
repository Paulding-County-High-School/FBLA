About Us

Who we are?

Paulding County High School's FBLA, is a national student business association club.

Our mission is to bring business and education together in a positive working relationship through innovative leadership and career development programs. FBLA, is the Career and Technology Student organization for students enrolled in the Business & Technology and the Web & Digital Design Pathways at Paulding County High School. Courses within these pathways include a Georgia Performance standard which states, “Explore how related student organizations are integral parts of career and technology education courses through leadership development, school and community service projects, entrepreneurship development, and competitive events”. Membership in this student organization will expose the students to several opportunities to develop leadership skills, network with other students across the state, and participate in competitive events.


High school students that join FBLA for $20 to receive benefits that include:

Leadership Development
Academic competitions
Community service
Awards & recognition
And more

Our Need for Partnerships

We are seeking to grow our local advisory board to provide students with opportunities for success in the workforce by engaging business partners for many roles: guest speakers, job shadowing, student conferences, mentorships, and internships. These types of activities can make learning more relevant by providing real-world examples.


Due to the lack of exposure to the business world, many students at Paulding County High School do not choose to pursue business/technology classes or clubs. Also, in a tough economy, many of our students lack the financial resources to pay for clubs that give them the opportunity to go on field trips. FBLA introduces our kids to the business and technology world as well and assist with the financial resources to attend field trips and workshops. That’s why fundraising is essential to the success of our FBLA program.


If you would like to join our Advisory board, please contact us via email. Also, if you would like to donate to our club, please go to our donation page.
